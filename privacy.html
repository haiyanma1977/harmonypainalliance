Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 20, 2026 · Last Updated: May 20, 2026

Plain-English summary: We collect only what you give us through our contact form (name, email, phone, and the condition you'd like help with) so we can connect you with the right clinic. We don't sell your data, we don't track you across the web, and you can ask us to delete your information at any time by emailing founder@harmonypainalliance.com.

1. Who We Are

Harmony Pain Alliance ("HPA," "we," "us," or "our") is a service operated by Harmony TCM International Group LLC, a Florida limited liability company. HPA is a connecting platform — we help patients in North America find acupuncture clinics that specialize in advanced techniques. We do not provide direct medical services; all clinical care is delivered independently by member clinics.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you fill out a contact, booking, or partnership form on harmonypainalliance.com. This typically includes:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • Your preferred contact method (email, phone, or WhatsApp)
  • The condition or service you're inquiring about
  • Your preferred language (English, Spanish, or Chinese)
  • For clinic partnership inquiries: clinic name, location, and credential information

We also store a small piece of data in your browser ("localStorage") to remember your language preference. This stays on your device and is not sent to us.

We do not currently use third-party advertising cookies, retargeting pixels, or cross-site tracking.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry
  • Forward your contact details to the relevant member clinic so they can schedule your visit
  • Send you appointment-related follow-up communications
  • Improve our service and the clinics we recommend

4. How We Share Your Information

We share your information only with the parties needed to deliver the service you requested:

  • Member clinics — When you request a referral or booking, we forward your contact information and stated condition to the clinic you selected (e.g., Lei's Acupuncture). The clinic then contacts you directly to schedule your visit and handles any health information you choose to share with them under their own privacy practices and applicable healthcare regulations.
  • Booking platform — Member clinics may use third-party scheduling platforms such as Jane App. When you book through such platforms, their separate privacy policy applies.
  • Legal compliance — We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, or court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of HPA, our users, or others.

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Health Information

HPA is not a covered entity under the U.S. Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). The contact form on our website is intended for general inquiries — please do not submit detailed medical records, diagnoses, or sensitive health information through it. Discuss your medical history directly with the licensed clinician at the clinic you choose to visit; their handling of your protected health information is governed by HIPAA and the clinic's own privacy practices.

6. Data Retention

We retain inquiry information for as long as needed to respond to your request and for a reasonable period afterward to maintain referral records and improve our service. You may request deletion at any time by emailing us.

7. Your Rights

Regardless of where you live, you may:

  • Request a copy of the information we hold about you
  • Request that we correct inaccurate information
  • Request that we delete your information
  • Opt out of future communications

To exercise any of these rights, email founder@harmonypainalliance.com. We'll respond within 30 days. Residents of California, Colorado, Virginia, and other U.S. states with consumer-privacy statutes have the same rights under those laws.

8. Security

We use industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your information in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we will notify affected users of any material data breach as required by applicable law.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us, please email us and we will delete it.

10. International Users

HPA is operated from the United States. If you access our service from outside the U.S., your information will be transferred to, processed in, and stored in the United States.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated through a notice on our website.
